ABOUT US

We love coding!

Welcome to the Kode with Klossy advice page! Coming into Kode with Klossy, you may not know what to expect or how to thrive in such an environment. Here, you can get customized advice from KWK alumni on what to expect, things you will learn, and much more. This website only provides a taste of what you will experience on your Kode With Klossy journey. As you may already know, Kode With Klossy is a program created by supermodel Karlie Kloss, whose goal is to help close the gender gap in STEM studies and careers. In this program, girls aged 13-18 are immersed in a fast-paced two-week coding crash course.
